Privacy Policy.

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective: March 2026

infoOverview

Tussle.AI ("Tussle", "we", "us", "our") builds real-time sales intelligence software that listens to your sales calls, calculates margins, and surfaces negotiation guidance while you speak.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We take privacy seriously — your data is a weapon in your hands, not ours.

By using Tussle.AI or joining our waitlist, you agree to the terms of this policy.

databaseInformation We Collect

When you join our waitlist:

  • Email address (required to notify you of beta access)
  • Name (optional, if provided)
  • Role and company (optional, if provided)
  • How you heard about us (optional)

When you use the Tussle product (beta):

  • Audio streams from your sales calls (processed in real-time, not stored unless you opt in)
  • Pricing parameters you configure (floor price, target price, margin %)
  • Interaction data — which suggestions you acted on, session duration, feature usage
  • Integration data from connected tools (Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Salesforce, HubSpot)

Automatically collected data:

  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level)
  • Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Page views, referral source, session duration
  • Crash reports and error logs

track_changesHow We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to operate and improve Tussle.AI. Specifically:

  • To notify you when your beta spot is ready
  • To deliver real-time negotiation guidance during your calls
  • To personalize the product to your deal parameters and selling style
  • To improve our AI models — only with your explicit opt-in consent
  • To send product updates, tips, and announcements (unsubscribe anytime)
  • To troubleshoot bugs and improve reliability
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Ever.

micCall Audio & AI Processing

Tussle's core product listens to your sales calls to provide real-time intelligence. Here's exactly how we handle that:

  • Real-time processing: Audio is processed live through our AI pipeline to generate suggestions. It is not stored on our servers by default.
  • No permanent recording: We do not record or retain call audio after a session ends unless you explicitly enable "Call Memory" in your settings.
  • Your deal data stays yours: Floor prices, targets, and margin figures you enter are stored securely and used only to power your session.
  • AI training opt-in: We may ask if you'd like to contribute anonymised interaction patterns to improve our models. This is always opt-in, never automatic.
  • Consent reminder: You are responsible for ensuring all parties on your calls are aware of and consent to AI analysis as required by applicable law in your jurisdiction.

shareThird-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party providers to operate our infrastructure. These providers access only the minimum data necessary:

  • Vercel — hosting and content delivery
  • Supabase / AWS — secure database and cloud infrastructure
  • OpenAI / Anthropic — AI inference for real-time suggestions (data is not retained by these providers for training)
  • Mailchimp / ConvertKit — email communications (waitlist notifications, product updates)
  • Plausible / PostHog — privacy-first analytics (no cross-site tracking, no fingerprinting)

All third-party providers are contractually required to handle your data in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

lockData Security

We take security seriously — it's non-negotiable.

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access to production systems is restricted to essential personnel only
  • We perform regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • We maintain incident response procedures and will notify affected users within 72 hours of any breach
  • We are working toward SOC 2 Type II compliance

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@tussle.ai.

scheduleData Retention

  • Waitlist data: Retained until you request deletion or 24 months after product launch, whichever comes first
  • Session/call data: Not retained beyond the active session unless Call Memory is enabled
  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 30 days after deletion request
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymised, retained indefinitely
  • Legal hold: Some data may be retained longer if required by applicable law

verified_userYour Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for direct marketing
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@tussle.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

cookieCookies

We use minimal, essential cookies to operate the site. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

  • Session cookies: Required for authentication and keeping you logged in
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings
  • Analytics: We use Plausible Analytics — no cookies, no fingerprinting, GDPR-compliant by default

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not function correctly without essential cookies.
